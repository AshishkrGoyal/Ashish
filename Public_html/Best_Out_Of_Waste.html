Best Out Of Waste

Students were invited to prepare any object they want (like utilitarian or decorative objects, accessories, furniture articles, showpieces, etc.), from any waste material that was provided to them, using their inspiration, ideas and creativity, understanding that waste material is something found in the garbage or something that is normally intended to be thrown away. The basic goal behind the event was to make the participants aware about preserving the environment and moreover learn more about sustainability.
